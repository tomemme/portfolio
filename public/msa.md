# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT
This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement" or "MSA") is entered into as of [Date], by and between Tech Integration Solutions LLC ("Consultant") and [Client Company Name] ("Client").

### 1. Scope of Agreement & Statements of Work
This Agreement governs the overall professional relationship between the parties. The specific engineering, automation, or consulting services to be performed will be described in individual, mutually executed Statements of Work ("SOW"). Each SOW will reference this MSA and define the specific deliverables, timelines, and compensation. If a conflict arises between this MSA and an SOW, the SOW will control for that specific project.

### 2. Independent Contractor Status
Consultant performs all services solely as an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, employer-employee, or agency relationship. Consultant retains absolute control over the hours worked, methods, tools, and technical processes used to achieve the deliverables specified in an SOW. Client will not provide employee benefits, workers' compensation, or withhold taxes; Client will issue a Form 1099 for tax reporting purposes.

### 3. Payment Terms, Late Fees, & Risk Control
* Invoicing: Invoices will be issued according to the milestone or payment schedule defined in the applicable SOW. Client agrees to pay all undisputed invoices within fourteen (14) days of the invoice date.

* Late Payment Penalties: Any invoice remaining unpaid past the due date will accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month, or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.

* Suspension of Service: If an invoice remains unpaid for more than thirty (30) days, Consultant reserves the absolute right to suspend all active development, pipeline management, deployment, or support environments without liability, upon three (3) days' written notice, until all outstanding balances are paid in full.

### 4. Intellectual Property & Ownership of Deliverables
* Client Custom Deliverables: Upon full and final payment of all outstanding fees related to an SOW, Consultant assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in the custom code, scripts, specific database configurations, and documentation created explicitly for Client under that SOW.

* Consultant Pre-Existing IP & Background Tooling: Consultant retains sole ownership of all pre-existing tools, core software architectures, proprietary libraries, scripts, automation frameworks, open-source modifications, and code snippets utilized or developed independently of this Agreement (collectively, "Background IP"). Consultant grants Client a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free, perpetual license to use any Background IP embedded within the deliverables solely for Client’s internal business operations.

### 5. Third-Party Tools, APIs, and AI Usage
* Third-Party Infrastructure: Deliverables may rely on third-party APIs, cloud platforms (e.g., AWS), or software services. Client is solely responsible for maintaining active, funded accounts with these providers. Consultant is not liable for system failures, service modifications, rate-limiting, or downtime caused by these external platforms.

* AI Tooling & Deployed Logic: Consultant utilizes advanced artificial intelligence tools and automated code generation environments to maximize development efficiency and build speed. Client acknowledges and consents to the use of these technical workflows in the engineering of deliverables.

### 6. Warranties & Limitations of Liability
* Warranty Disclaimer: Consultant warrants that services will be performed in a professional, workmanlike manner matching enterprise automation standards. EXCEPT FOR THIS LIMITED WARRANTY, ALL SERVICES AND DELIVERABLES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS." CONSULTANT DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE OR UNINTERRUPTED PIPELINE OPERATION.

* Limitation of Liability: TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PERMITTED BY LAW, IN NO EVENT WILL CONSULTANT BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOSS OF REVENUE, DATA, REPUTATION, OR BUSINESS INTERRUPTION. CONSULTANT’S TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY ARISING OUT OF THIS AGREEMENT OR ANY SOW WILL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES ACTUALLY PAID BY CLIENT TO CONSULTANT UNDER THE SPECIFIC SOW GIVING RISE TO THE LIABILITY.

### 7. Change Requests & Scope Boundaries
Any request by Client to alter the scope, technical requirements, dashboard parameters, or data pipelines defined in an active SOW must be submitted in writing. Consultant will evaluate the request and provide an estimated impact on cost and timeline. No changes will take effect until both parties execute a written Change Order or a new SOW.

### 8. Term and Termination
* Term: This MSA commences on the effective date and remains in effect until terminated by either party.

* Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate this MSA or an active SOW for convenience upon thirty (30) days' written notice, provided that Client pays Consultant in full for all work completed up to the effective termination date.

* Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately if the other party breaches a material term and fails to cure such breach within ten (10) days of receiving written notice.

### 9. Dispute Resolution & Governing Law
This Agreement will be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Oklahoma, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Any legal action, dispute, or proceeding arising out of this Agreement must be brought exclusively in the state or federal courts located in Oklahoma, and both parties consent to personal jurisdiction there.
