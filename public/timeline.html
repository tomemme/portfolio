My Technology Timeline

A chronological journey through my key milestones in technology.

'90s

Got my first PC and gaming console

Started tinkering around with a PC my aunt gifted me. Big fan of the Game Boy and PS1, which sparked my interest in technology.

2001

Bought my first MP3 player

Having a love for music, and wanting to try new tech, I moved on from CD's.

2008

First Laptop, PC & Xbox in the Navy

My roommate ran Windows on a MacBook to play specific games, interest peaked. Began exploring dual-boot setups and hardware compatibility.

2009

Data Analyst at Naval Medical Center San Diego

Analyzed patient satisfaction surveys in SPSS, generated data reports for command review, and improved decision-making for hospital staff performance.

2009

CompTIA Security+ & ISSO Role

Passed Security+ certification. Became the department's ISSO, triaged minor tech issues with Hospital IT, strengthening my cybersecurity foundation.

2009

Built Intranet page for Credentialing

Volunteered to create an intranet page to organize doctors' credential paperwork, scanned hundreds of documents, streamlined verification, and reduced phone inquiries.

2009

Started First YouTube Channel

Recorded COD4 gameplay with a capture card, edited in Pinnacle Studio. Learned Xbox repair and bypass techniques from local contacts, sparked a “hacker mindset.”

2010

Explored Jailbreaking iPhones & Xbox

Dug into the mechanics of jailbreaking, understood the steps teams take to prevent it. This fueled my curiosity about firmware, exploits, and reverse engineering.

2015

First OOP Class in C#

Switched from nursing courses to pursue technology. Learned object-oriented principles in C#, which solidified my passion for software development.

2016

Began Deep Dive into Bitcoin

Researched Bitcoin's inner workings—studied blockchain mechanics, cryptographic hashing, and decentralized consensus algorithms.

2016

Completed Community College

Earned my Associate's in Computer Information Systems, building a strong foundation in networking, databases, and basic programming concepts.

2017

Jolt Hackathon Participation

Attended my first hackathon, built out-of-the-box thinking skills through Capture the Flag challenges and collaborative problem solving.

2017

Arkansas Coding Academy (ACA)

Battled imposter syndrome and completed a 3 month Java/SQL bootcamp—strengthened my coding muscles and learned best practices for software design.

2017

Dev/Ops Internship at Merkle & Return to UNI

Landed a Dev/Ops internship—worked with Linux, PowerShell, and BASH. Returned to university to finish my Information Science degree.

2018

Built First Gaming PC

Planned, sourced parts, and built my own gaming PC, transitioned fully to PC gaming and learned about hardware benchmarking and overclocking.

2020

Graduated & Hired as Developer

Completed my degree and joined as a developer. Over the previous years, I honed skills in PowerShell, Linux, T-SQL, interface design, C++, Lisp, JavaScript, Python, Flutter/Dart, and more.

2022

AI Enhancement Begins

With ChatGPT's release, I dove into AI—built tools, automated workflows, and launched CryptoTidbits (an AI driven YouTube channel) using Python and powershell scripts.

2025

AI Enhancement 2.0

Leveraging open source models on local hardware—rapidly prototyping multi-language AI projects. Turning big ideas into working products faster than ever imagined.
